The Travel Diaries

Arrive in Brisbane - February 5th, 2023

Paris is a beautiful city with so much history and culture. I spent a week there exploring all the famous landmarks, including the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre, and Notre-Dame Cathedral.

One of the highlights of my trip was watching the sunset from the top of the Eiffel Tower. The view was absolutely breathtaking!

I also spent a full day at the Louvre Museum, which houses some of the most famous works of art in the world, including the Mona Lisa.

Tokyo, Japan - November 2022

Tokyo is a bustling metropolis with a unique blend of modern and traditional culture. During my visit, I experienced everything from traditional tea ceremonies to high-tech shopping malls.

One of my favorite experiences was visiting the Tokyo Skytree, which is the tallest tower in the world. The view from the top was simply stunning!

I also visited Shibuya Crossing, which is one of the busiest intersections in the world. It was a surreal experience being surrounded by so many people!
