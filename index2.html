Flat Rock Camping

March 17th - 19th

Located about 45 minutes south of Byron Bay in NSW, this was the first camping trip I took part of with the UQ Surfers Club. The swell that weekend wasn't impressive, however that was all made up for on the final morning when a pod of a dozen dolphins joined us in the water, showcasing their wave-riding abilities to our admiration. Aside from many hours of wave-chasing we took a drive up to a lake near Lennox Heads, watched a movie under the stars, ate around the campfire and got some over-priced pub feed nearby.
